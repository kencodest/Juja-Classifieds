How to Avoid Scams

Deal locally and insist on face-to-face interactions

Follow this rule and avoid 99% of scam attempts.

Do not provide payment

Do not give any payments to anyone you have not met in person.

Beware of offers involving shipping costs

Our website caters to Juja residents only.

Never wire funds

Do not trust any vendors who require payment before providing proof of existence.

Vendors do not accept cashier's checks or money orders

Banks often cash fakes then hold you responsible.

Transactions are between sellers and buyers only

No third party can provide a "guarantee". This is probably a scam.

Never give out your financial information

This includes bank account pins, social security number, or credentials to other accounts like paypal or payoneer.

Do not rent or purchase sites you are yet to verify

Always remember, that amazing "deal" may not exist.

Refuse background checks

until you have met landlord/employer in person.

Juja Classifieds voicemails

Any message asking you to access or check "Juja Classifieds voice messages" is fraudulent - no such service exists.
